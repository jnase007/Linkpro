ELDOA & Myofascial Stretching

Focusing on spinal health is imperative for maintaining basic functions as we age. If you're sticky, tight, or compressed in your spine, pelvis, and hips — your body ages itself. We got you.

What is ELDOA

Decompressing the spine with extraordinary precision

ELDOA stands for Étirements Longitudinaux avec Décoaptation Ostéo-Articulaire — a type of exercise that focuses on creating space within the joints of the spine and other areas of the body.

These deceptively simple positions, held for one minute each, produce extraordinary results — decompressing the spine, improving posture, and accelerating recovery.

  • Spinal Health: Relieves back and neck pain by reducing pressure on discs and nerves
  • Improved Posture: Promotes better alignment over time
  • Increased Flexibility: Enhances overall flexibility and mobility
  • Core Strength: Strengthens deep muscles that support the spine
  • Stress Relief: Focused breathing and stretching reduces tension
