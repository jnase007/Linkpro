Treatment Modalities

Our Services

A comprehensive suite of specialized services designed to address root causes, optimize performance, and promote long-term health.

Comprehensive Consultations

We begin with a thorough evaluation of your body's structure, posture, and fluid dynamics to identify the root cause of pain or dysfunction. This global assessment serves as the foundation for crafting personalized treatment plans tailored to your specific goals — whether it's pain relief, performance enhancement, or improved overall health.

Manual Osteopathy

Our team utilizes advanced osteopathic techniques, focusing on the interconnectedness of the body's fascia, bones, and fluids.

  • Joint Pumping: Restores fluidity and movement within joints and surrounding tissues, promoting healing and reducing pain.
  • Fascial Therapy: Targets deep, middle, and superficial fascia layers to release tension and improve function.
  • Visceral Manipulation: Enhances organ mobility and function through the intricate relationships between the visceral and musculoskeletal systems.

ELDOA & Myofascial Stretching

Specialized group classes focused on improving posture, relieving pain, and restoring movement through targeted stretching and strengthening techniques.

  • ELDOA: Aimed at decompressing specific areas of the spine to alleviate tension and improve alignment.
  • Myofascial Stretching: Enhances mobility by releasing tension within the fascia, improving overall flexibility and functional movement.

Post-Injury & Post-Surgical Recovery

Specialized recovery programs that incorporate hydrotherapy, manual techniques, and personalized exercises to accelerate healing and restore function.

  • Hydrotherapy: Reconditioning in water to reduce load on injured areas while improving mobility and strength.
  • Rehydration Therapy: Restores fluid dynamics in tissues to support healing and long-term recovery.

Concussion & Neurological Rehabilitation

Our comprehensive approach addresses brain and balance issues by focusing on key anatomical relationships — the pelvis, spine, and cranial system.

  • Integration of the four diaphragms (thoracic, pelvic, cervical, and cranial) to improve neurological function.
  • Exercises tailored to enhance eye tracking, vestibular function, and cerebellar performance.

Pediatric Osteopathy

Specialized care for newborns and children, addressing issues such as skull development, hip alignment, and early motor function. This gentle, water-based approach identifies and resolves imbalances before they affect long-term growth and development.

Sports Performance Optimization

Using a combination of manual therapy, movement analysis, and exercise prescription, we help athletes unlock their full potential. From preventing injuries to improving biomechanics for running, throwing, and other sport-specific movements, our team provides tools for peak performance.

Educational Workshops & Courses

Dr. Amstutz offers a range of courses for professionals, athletes, and health enthusiasts.

  • SomaTherapy & SomaTraining Courses: Advanced training in manual osteopathy and exercise techniques.
  • Snapshot Classes: Short, focused workshops addressing specific challenges like back pain or postural imbalances.
  • Team & Organization Training: Customized education for sports teams or healthcare groups.
