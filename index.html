Group Classes

ELDOA &
Myofascial Stretching

ELDOA (Étirements Longitudinaux avec Décoaptation Ostéo-Articulaire) uses precise postures held for one minute to create space in targeted joints. These deceptively simple positions produce extraordinary results — decompressing the spine, improving posture, and accelerating recovery.

Our group classes combine ELDOA with myofascial stretching techniques to restore fascial fluidity and enhance overall tissue quality. Suitable for all levels, from elite athletes to those beginning their wellness journey.
